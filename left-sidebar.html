Marine Engineering Systems Operator

Canadian Armed Forces

Being apart of the Royal Canadian Navy is truly an honor. As a Marine Engineer in training, i have developed the knowledge to maintain and design advanced marine engineering eqipment and systems. These systems range from electrical systems to fuel/propulsion systems, and all the way to lubrication/steering systems. I have the ability to duplicate and create similar systems myself using AutoCAD. Knowing these systems requires a detailed expertise in different types of valves, switches, pumps, gauges and other major components.
